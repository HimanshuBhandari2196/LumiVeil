How it works

From suspicious to certain
in three steps

No switching tabs, no manual searching. LumiVeil works silently in your browser and speaks up when it matters.

01
Browse normally
Install the extension once. LumiVeil runs quietly in the background on every page you visit — no action needed from you.
02
Content gets analysed
Within 2 seconds of page load, LumiVeil checks the URL against 100+ trusted domains, scans for sensational language, and inspects images for AI generation or deepfakes.
03
You get a verdict
If the trust score drops below 40, a warning banner slides in at the top of the page. Click "View full report" for a detailed breakdown of every check performed.

In development
Video deepfake detection
Unlike images, video deepfakes hide their artifacts in the motion between frames — not in any single frame. This makes detection fundamentally harder and more computationally expensive. We're researching temporal analysis techniques and evaluating APIs that go beyond frame-by-frame sampling. Contributions and ideas welcome.
